East Coast Bays is a ward in the North Shore, a city in greater Auckland. It includes the suburbs of (south to north) Mairangi Bay, Murray's Bay, Rothesay Bay, Browns Bay, Torbay and Long Bay.
There are three beaches in the northern locality, Browns Bay, Long Bay and Waiake or Torbay. There is a wide selection of shops at Browns bay which are close to the beach and make a pleasant break from the sea front. Long Bay and Waiake both have restaurants. There is also a smattering of shops including a dairy on the approach to Long bay. Waiake is a little more removed.
There are numerous takeaways at Browns bay and on the approach road to Long Bay. There are regular council ‘fun days’ and local event which can be found at ‘eventfinder.co.nz’.
